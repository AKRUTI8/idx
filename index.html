Why should I measure facial expressions?

Facial expression analysis provides insights into people's emotions, behaviors, and decision-making. It's an important part of emotion analysis

When to include facial expressions

You may want to include facial expression analysis in your work if you're studying consumer behavior or user experience.

It's also the perfect choice for research on social interactions, developmental psychology, or mental illness.

What can you measure with FaceReader?

FaceReaderTM analyzes the six universal expressions: happy, sad, angry, surprised, scared, and disgusted, plus contempt and a neutral state.

How to expand your setup

You can also include Action Unit measurements and define your own custom expressions. Other options include measuring heart rate, eating and drinking behavior, or infants expressions.
